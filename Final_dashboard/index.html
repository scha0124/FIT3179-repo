Population growth from 2015 to 2021

Philippines has the highest population density, followed by Vietnam and Laos has the lowest population density.

Indonesia is about 3.7 times bigger than Thailand, but their population densities are close to each other.

The population densities of Singapore are not shown in this chart because the population densities are too large to be included.

Although Singapore is the smallest country among all ASEAN countries, but it has the highest population density.

Median Age of Population in 2021

Singapore has the highest median age of 42 years old due to an increase in life expectancy and decreasing birth rates.

From the research data in 2018, it was found that Singapore is the country with the highest life expectancies in the world. As of 2017, it was found that Singapore has the lowest fertility rate in the world, at only 0.83 children per woman.

Thailand has the second highest median age, which is 40 years old.

Indonesia and Malaysia have the same median age of 30 years old whereas Brunei and Vietnam also have the same median age of 32 years old.
